Waste Water Management
Project Summary

This working model demonstrates an accessible rain water management system that emphasises hygiene, water recycling and community health. It combines mechanical filtration, sedimentation, and a simple disinfection step to show how contaminated water can be treated for non-potable reuse. Designed to be demonstrative and educational for exhibition settings.
